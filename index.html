🎯 Tips for Successful Weight Loss

1. Set Realistic Goals

Aim for 0.5-1 kg weight loss per week. This is healthy and sustainable.

2. Track Everything

Log all meals, drinks, and exercises for accurate progress tracking.

3. Calorie Deficit

Consume 300-500 calories less than your daily requirement.

4. Stay Hydrated

Drink 2-3 liters of water daily. It helps with metabolism.

5. Exercise Regularly

Combine cardio (150 min/week) and strength training (2-3x/week).

6. Sleep Well

Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep for better recovery and metabolism.
